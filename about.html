About me

I like problems
that make other
people's heads
hurt.

I'm a product designer who likes to understand how things connect before jumping to solutions. In practice that means I map relationships, consider constraints, and genuinely enjoy the problems that make other people's heads hurt.

I came to product design through graphic design and marketing , which taught me how to communicate visually, read audiences, and navigate organizations. Product design was where those things clicked into place.

Background

I'm originally from the Philippines and I now live in Berlin, Germany, after spending nearly a decade in Ireland and a few years in Singapore. These days you'll find me hosting and organizing community events for UXDX, learning German, solving logic puzzles, making things, and exploring what AI-assisted prototyping means for designers.

I recently learned how to crochet, so there has been a lot of that too.
